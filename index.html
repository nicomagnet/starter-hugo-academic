Engineer, Researcher, Innovator

Ingeniero, Investigador, Innovador

I am a Battery Systems Engineer from Ecuador with a Ph.D. from University Grenoble Alpes, based in Paris. At SEGULA Technologies — Ampere (Renault Group), I validate and optimize battery state estimators within the GTR-EVE regulatory framework for electric vehicle durability.

My focus includes WLTP break-off optimization, BMS performance enhancement, and SOH estimation via EIS. Previously I led hardware development for smart transportation in Ecuador, conducted UAV energy research in Barcelona, and completed a Ph.D. on Li-ion battery performance at LEPMI — Grenoble INP.

I hold a patent in battery state estimation and was a NASA Space Apps Challenge local winner.

Based in Paris, I'm passionate about advancing sustainable mobility through smarter battery systems. Open to collaborations in BMS development, durability analysis, and energy storage innovation.

Basé à Paris, je suis passionné par l'avancement de la mobilité durable grâce à des systèmes batterie plus intelligents. Ouvert aux collaborations en développement BMS, analyse de durabilité et innovation en stockage d'énergie.

Con sede en París, me apasiona avanzar la movilidad sostenible mediante sistemas de batería más inteligentes. Abierto a colaboraciones en desarrollo BMS, análisis de durabilidad e innovación en almacenamiento de energía.
